We provide "Safe Deal" services for the purchase and sale and for the provision / receipt of services. Using our public wallets on open blockchain and stablecoins : USDT, USDC, PAXG ( Pax Gold ), XAUt (Tether Gold) .


When making a deal , reserve your payment on the cBS5M public wallet. We will announce your trade and once your partner completes the terms of the trade, the reserved funds will go to your partner's public wallet.


If your partner does not fulfill the terms of your deal, your funds will be returned to your public wallet. In the event of a controversial situation, we study the evidence and facts, participants and other details. It is possible to consider the dispute at the public council.

By using this service, you acknowledge that you have read and are following the rules of this community. In case of a controversial situation, you agree with the decision of the company and the cBS5M community or involve arbitration for an open investigation at your expense.
